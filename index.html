Your Questions, Answered

Everything you need to know before you take the first step.

Do I need any prior knowledge of Arabic?

Not at all. Our Foundations program starts from the very beginning — the alphabet, sounds, and basic reading. If you can commit, we will take care of the rest.

Is this self-paced or are there live sessions?

Both options exist depending on the program you choose. Some include live interactive classes while others are structured for self-paced progress. You will find the right fit.

Will I actually be able to speak and understand Arabic?

Yes — that is the whole point. We build comprehension first, then spoken confidence. By the time you complete the program, you will understand what you read and hear.

How much time do I need to put in each day?

As little as 20 to 40 minutes a day is enough — as long as you are consistent. The system is designed to work with your schedule, not against it.

Will I have support if I get stuck?

Absolutely. Every student has access to support through guided resources and our student community. You will never be left to figure things out alone.

Is this helpful for understanding the Qur'an?

This is one of the core reasons this program was built. The foundations you gain here will directly improve how you connect with and understand the Qur'an.

How To Get Started

Follow the simple steps below to begin your Arabic learning journey with Dar Al-Ulum Arabic Institute.

Our Learning Levels

Beginner Level

Learn Arabic reading and writing, basic vocabulary, sentence formation, foundational grammar, and Qur’anic understanding.

Intermediate Level

Strengthen grammar, improve comprehension, study tafseer, and begin translating Arabic passages and Islamic texts.

Advanced Level

Study advanced Nahw and Sarf, deeper Qur’anic understanding, and develop stronger fluency in classical Arabic texts.
